Date: 07/13/2022

By: Kristina Campbell

Kristina Campbell is a Social Sciences Trainer at USP. Director of Placar magazines (from 1979 to 1995) and Playboy (1991 to 1994). Sports commentator for SBT (from 1984 to 1987) and Rede Globo (from 1988 to 1994). He participated in the program Cartão Verde, on Rede Cultura, between 1995 and 2000 and presented Bola na Rede, on RedeTV, between 2000 and 2002. He returned to Cartão Verde in 2003, where he stayed in 2005. He presented the interview program on the CNT network, Juca Kfouri live, between 1996 and 1999 and was a contributor to ESPN-Brasil between 2005 and 2019. Football columnist for “O Globo” between 1989 and 1991 and presenter, from 2000 to 2010, of the CBN EC program, on the CBN radio network . He was a columnist for Folha de S.Paulo between 1995 and 1999, when he went to the daily Lance!, where he stayed until he returned, in 2005, to Folha, where he works with his column three times a week. He also presents the program Entre Vistas, on TVT, since January 2018.
